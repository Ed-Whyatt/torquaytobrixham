Long Quarry Point To Brixham Harbour Light House

I have recently pushed my love for hiking to new levels, pushing myself further and further. I think it all began from a young age growing up in a small village called Castle Eaton in the Cotswolds. My friends and i would always be going on little adventures across the footpaths, fields, and river Thems.

Me hikeing the SWCP.

So, on my visit to my mother's in Brixham, i decided to walk some of the South West Costal Path (SWCP) that runs 630 miles. Starting at Long Quarry Point and ending in Brixham. I have walked very little of this path and hope to do most of it in the future before i am no longer able to do so.
